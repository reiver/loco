# loco

**loco** is an application, in the form of a command-line tool, that enables users to __install__, __run__, __manage__, and __maintain__ **self-hosted** software as Docker containers.
**loco** is a tool designed for users with a **home-lab** — including those who want to use **loco** 

The **loco** tool aims to provide a turnkey experience for newcomers while giving power users full control.

**loco** has its origins with hosting Fediverse software.
But (is not limited to it and), can be used to for other types of software, too.

## Motivation

**loco** was primarily created for **self-hosters**.
To help make the act of __self-hosting__ easier.

**loco** was created by someone with years of experience at a hosting company in a commercial settings, and with years of experience with self-hosting (going back to the 1990s).
**loco** incorporates many lessons gained from those experiences.
